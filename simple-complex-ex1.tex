Left hand side
<p>
$$(3+2i)z$$
$$= (3+2i)(x + iy)$$
$$= (3x-2y)+i(2x+3y)$$

Right hand side
<p>
$$2i\overline{z}-5i$$
$$= 2i(x-iy)-5i$$
$$= 2ix+2y-5i$$
$$= 2y + i(2x-5)$$

Left hand side = Right hand side
<p>

$$(3x-2y)+i(2x+3y) = 2y + i(2x-5)$$
$$(3x-2y-2y)+i(2x+3y-2x+5) = 0$$
$$(3x-4y)+i(3y+5) = 0$$
