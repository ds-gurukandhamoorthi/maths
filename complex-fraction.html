Left hand side

$$\overline{\bigg(\frac{z}{z'}\bigg)}$$ $$ = \overline{\bigg(\frac{x+iy}{x'+iy'}\bigg)}$$ $$ = \overline{\bigg(\frac{(x+iy)(x'-iy')}{(x'+iy')(x'-iy')}\bigg)}$$ $$ = \overline{\bigg(\frac{(x+iy)(x'-iy')}{(x'^2+y'^2)}\bigg)}$$ $$ = \overline{\bigg(\frac{xx'+yy'+i(x'y-xy')}{(x'^2+y'^2)}\bigg)}$$ $$ = \frac{xx'+yy'-i(x'y-xy')}{(x'^2+y'^2)}$$ Right hand side

$$\frac{\overline{z}}{\enspace \overline{z'} \enspace}$$ $$ = \frac{\overline{x+iy}}{\enspace \overline{x'+iy'} \enspace}$$ $$ = \frac{x-iy}{\enspace x'-iy' \enspace}$$ $$ = \frac{(x-iy)(x'+iy')}{\enspace (x'-iy')(x'+iy') \enspace}$$ $$ = \frac{xx'+yy'-i(x'y-xy')}{\enspace (x'^2+y'^2) \enspace}$$
